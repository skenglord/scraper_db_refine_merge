About Ibiza Spotlight's Calendar

This calendar is recognised as the official Ibiza Party Calendar, with day-by-day updated listings of all clubs and DJs and tickets for all the major clubs.

Here are some popular dates: the club opening parties happen at the end of April and continue into May, the club closing parties are usually between the end of September and the middle of October. The Radio 1 weekend is usually the first weekend in August.

We update this calendar on a continuous basis so keep checking back. We do not copy dates from any other calendar or unverifiable news source.
