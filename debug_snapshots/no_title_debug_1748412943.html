Float Your Boat | Ibiza Sunset Experience - Boat Parties - San Antonio - 2025

At Boat Parties - San Antonio. Floating effortlessly towards the horizon line.

On this page

  • Events & djs
  • Events & djs

Having hosted boat parties on Ibiza for many years, Float Your Boat (FYB) is back with attitude for 2025. This year, FYB's adult-only boats start setting-off twice a week in peak summer.

Sailing from 18:00 to 21:00 (17:00 to 20:00 in September) from San Antonio harbour, FYB Ibiza Sunset Experience is a three hour excursion along Ibiza's west coast. You'll be taken on a musical journey through Chill Out, Balearic House, Ibiza club classics and future dancefloor hits.

The Ibiza Sunset Experience starts at a tranquil pace, mingling, seeing the sights before warming-up to some dancefloor action powered by a custom built Funktion One sound system. As the sun gets lower in the sky, the tempo increases as the DJ moves through the gears.

Float Your Boat starts sailing on Saturdays from 3 May, before going bi-weekly when Tuesdays are added from 7 June. The final sailing is set for 30 September.

See below for tickets.

Float Your Boat Ibiza Sunset Experience includes:

- three hour boat party with the world famous Ibiza sunset
- two cups of sangria as welcome drinks
- fully licenced bar with a wide variety of drinks, all at reasonable prices
- international DJs playing the tunes on board, accompanied by live bongo player
- Custom built Funktion One and Pioneer XY Pro-Audio sound system
- entry to O Beach before 14:00 either on Monday (D4 D4NCE) or Wednesdays (House In Paradise) and entry to Es Paradis nightclub - speak to staff on the day to claim your wristbands and discuss your options

Important information - please read

  • Meeting point and ticket redemption is at the Float Your Boat kiosk on the promenade, opposite the fountains and close to the egg roundabout (see map), from 17:00 onwards (16:00 in September) latest 17:30 (16:30 in September)
  • Please arrive early, with your ticket ready to show on your phone or printed out
  • The boat party runs approx. from 18:00 to 21:00 (17:00 to 20:00 in September)
  • Ticket includes the boat party and two welcome drinks of sangria
  • Ticket holders can also claim wristbands for free entry to either D4 D4NCE (Monday) or House In Paradise (Wednesday) at O Beach (both before 14:00)
  • Ticket holders can also claim free entry to a night at Es Paradis nightclub - speak to party organisers about your options. Terms and conditions apply.
  • People who are drunk will not be allowed to board. Enjoy yourselves, but please drink moderately. If anybody is clearly drinking too much on board, then we will be obliged to refuse service.
  • Coming on board is at your own risk. The vendor accepts no responsibility for any injuries sustained while on board, although the boat is fully insured under Spanish law. We expect responsible behaviour at all times while at sea.
  • Float Your Boat is committed to promoting sensible and responsible drinking, and anyone not adhering to this will not be allowed to board the boats or receive a refund
  • Float Your Boat reserves the right to reschedule the boat party to another time within 24 hours with no refund, if the boat company deems weather or mechanical problems make it impossible for boats to sail
  • If you arrive to the boat after above times, you will not be allowed to board the boat. Please arrive promptly.
  • The sale and/or use of drugs is strictly prohibited under Spanish law
